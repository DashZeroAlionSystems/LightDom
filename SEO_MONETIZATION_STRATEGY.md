## 4. Lock-in Mechanisms

### 4.1 Service Termination Effects
When customers stop paying, we automatically:
- Remove injected schema markup
- Disable rich snippets
- Stop monitoring and alerts
- Remove API access
- Archive historical data (retain for potential re-activation)

This creates strong incentive to maintain subscription.

### 4.2 Data Ownership
- Customers own their data
- We provide export functionality
- Historical data retained for 90 days post-cancellation
- Re-activation restores full history
